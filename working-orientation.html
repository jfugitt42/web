Working Orientation

These notes help me show up more reliably in life, especially when things get messy.

I’m aiming less for “confidence” and more for availability, staying present, responding honestly, and repairing quickly along the journey.

  • Stay closer to what’s happening than to the story about it. Attention over narration.
  • Act without requiring comfort, certainty, or approval. Recoverability matters more than confidence.
  • Be useful where possible; don’t disappear to be helpful. Contribution without erasure.
  • Speak what’s true enough for the moment. Clarity over performance.
  • Trust myself to respond and repair. No pre-negotiation with fear.

Re-read when stuck. Ignore when things are flowing.
