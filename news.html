Daily News

Core

  • Reuters World News — ten-minute weekday world news briefing
  • NPR Up First — concise morning headlines and reporting
  • BBC Global News Podcast — strong international daily coverage

More Daily Sources

  • NPR News Now — very short headline updates
  • Bloomberg Daybreak — markets, business, and economic context
  • FT News Briefing — business, policy, and international economic news
  • WSJ What’s News — daily headlines with a business and policy tilt

International

  • DW News Brief — Germany-based international news
  • France 24 Podcasts — world news from a France-based perspective
  • NHK World Japan Podcasts — Asia and world coverage from Japan
  • Radio New Zealand News — New Zealand public broadcaster reporting

Investigations and Long Form

  • ProPublica Podcasts — investigations and accountability reporting
  • The Atlantic Podcasts — essays, interviews, and deeper context
  • The Daily — deeper narrative reporting and interpretation

Suggested order

  • Reuters World News
  • NPR Up First
  • BBC Global News Podcast
  • Bloomberg Daybreak or FT News Briefing
  • One international source such as DW, France 24, NHK World, or Radio New Zealand
  • One long-form source such as ProPublica, The Atlantic, or The Daily

Notes

  • Reuters and NPR are good first reads for the day.
  • BBC, DW, France 24, NHK World, and Radio New Zealand help reduce U.S.-only framing.
  • Bloomberg, FT, and WSJ are especially useful for markets, business, and policy.
  • ProPublica and The Atlantic are better for depth than for rapid headline scanning.
