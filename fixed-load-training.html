FIXED LOAD TRAINING 
PURPOSE

Reach a defined level of strength early.
Maintain it unchanged for life.


ENTRY STANDARD

≈ 300 Wilks


EQUIPMENT

- Barbell
- 25 lb plates (one pair)
- 45 lb plates (up to four pairs)


LIFTS & ORDER

1. Squat
2. Bench Press
3. Pull-Up (weighted)
4. Overhead Press
5. Barbell Curl
6. Deadlift


DAILY STRUCTURE

Each lift:

- ~5 reps (≈60%)
- ~2–3 reps (≈80%)
- 1 clean single

Loads approximate. Use only 45s and 25s.


EXECUTION

- All reps controlled
- Singles repeatable
- No grinding

Pressing: shoulders down and back
Pull-ups: chest to bar, brief squeeze at top


FREQUENCY

Daily.


BODYWEIGHT

Maintain bodyweight ≤186 lbs.
Eat to recover, not to excess.


BELT

Optional.
Use only when needed.
