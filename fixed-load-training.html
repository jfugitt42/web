FIXED LOAD TRAINING
PURPOSE

Reach a defined level of strength early.
Maintain it unchanged for life.


ENTRY STANDARD

≈ 300 Wilks


EQUIPMENT

- Barbell
- 25 lb plates (one pair)
- 45 lb plates (up to four pairs)
- Belt (Optional, use only when needed)


STRUCTURE

Each lift:

- ~5 reps (≈60%)
- ~2–3 reps (≈80%)
- 1 clean single

Use only 45s and 25s.


EXECUTION

- All reps controlled
- Singles repeatable
- No grinding

Pressing: shoulders down and back
Pull-ups: chest to bar, brief squeeze at top


FREQUENCY

Daily.


BODYWEIGHT

Maintain bodyweight ≤186 lbs.
Eat to recover, not to excess.


STANDARD

Did you lift and run today?

No credit for past or planned days.


END STATE

A fixed level of strength,
maintained without change,
through daily practice.

LOAD REFERENCE

Bar = 45
25's = 50
45's = 90
