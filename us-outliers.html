Things Most Democracies Treat as Normal, But the U.S. Still Fights About

This is a broad, non-exhaustive catalog of policies, norms, and institutions that are widely settled or standardized across most advanced democracies, but remain unusually controversial, fragmented, or absent in the United States.

Healthcare & Social Protection

  • Universal healthcare coverage
  • Single-payer or national health-insurance systems
  • Universal access to primary care
  • Universal hospital coverage
  • Universal catastrophic coverage
  • Universal mental-health coverage
  • Universal dental coverage
  • Universal vision coverage
  • National long-term care insurance
  • Regulated hospital pricing
  • Regulated ambulance pricing
  • Strict regulation of prescription drug prices
  • National vaccine policy with broad consensus
  • National reproductive healthcare framework
  • Paid maternity leave
  • Paid paternity leave
  • Paid parental leave for both parents
  • Universal or heavily subsidized childcare
  • Universal pre-K or early childhood education
  • Universal child allowances or child benefit payments
  • Comprehensive elder-care support systems
  • Robust unemployment insurance
  • National disability insurance
  • Guaranteed sick leave
  • National maternity and parental protections at work

Elections, Voting, and Democratic Structure

  • Independent redistricting commissions
  • Ban on partisan gerrymandering
  • Proportional representation electoral systems
  • Multi-party parliamentary systems
  • Coalition governments as the norm
  • National popular-vote systems for executive offices
  • Automatic voter registration
  • Same-day voter registration
  • Universal national voter rolls
  • Universal mail-in or postal voting
  • Weekend or holiday voting
  • Mandatory or strongly expected voter participation
  • Short, legally limited campaign seasons
  • Public financing of campaigns
  • Strict caps on campaign donations
  • Strict caps on campaign spending
  • Limits on outside or dark money in politics
  • Ban or tight limits on corporate political donations
  • Ban or tight limits on foreign political donations
  • Strong campaign advertising regulations
  • Equal media access rules for parties during elections
  • Independent national election administration
  • Nationwide standardized voting procedures
  • Independent national election-monitoring bodies

Social Issues & Civil Rights

  • National abortion access framework
  • National contraception and reproductive rights policy
  • Comprehensive sex education in schools
  • Standardized, science-based health curricula
  • LGBTQ+ nondiscrimination protections nationwide
  • Same-sex marriage recognized nationally
  • Gender identity protections in employment
  • Gender identity protections in housing
  • Gender identity protections in public accommodations
  • National civil-rights protections that do not vary by region
  • Hate-crime legislation with clear enforcement
  • Hate-speech regulations or prohibitions
  • Secular public policy norms
  • Separation of religious institutions from state policymaking
  • Legal frameworks for recognizing nonbinary identities
  • Legal gender-change procedures in national law

Gun Policy & Public Safety

  • National gun licensing requirements
  • National background checks for all firearm purchases
  • Mandatory firearm training for ownership
  • Mandatory safe-storage rules for firearms
  • Mandatory reporting of lost or stolen firearms
  • National gun registry
  • Restrictions on high-capacity magazines
  • Restrictions or bans on military-style rifles
  • Restrictions on public carry of firearms
  • Strict limits on civilian handgun ownership
  • Routine, centralized gun-death data collection

Education

  • Tuition-free public universities
  • Very low-cost public universities
  • Public vocational and technical colleges
  • National curriculum standards
  • National graduation standards
  • National teacher-certification standards
  • Universal public preschool
  • Universal school meals or heavily subsidized school meals
  • Robust apprenticeship programs linked to employers
  • Strong funding guarantees for public schools
  • Limits on for-profit primary and secondary schools
  • Limits on student-loan interest rates
  • National student-debt relief or forgiveness schemes

Labor & Workplace

  • Legally guaranteed paid vacation
  • Legally guaranteed paid public holidays
  • Shorter standard full-time workweek (e.g., 35â€“40 hours)
  • Overtime pay requirements with strong enforcement
  • Legal protections against at-will termination
  • Requirement of just cause for firing employees
  • Strong severance protections
  • Strong workplace-safety regulations
  • National occupational health standards
  • Easy union recognition procedures
  • Sector-wide collective bargaining
  • High union density supported by law
  • Worker councils or works councils
  • Worker representation on corporate boards
  • Protections for gig and platform workers
  • Legal protections for domestic workers
  • Legal protections for migrant workers
  • Right-to-disconnect laws
  • National apprenticeship and training systems

Justice & Criminal Legal System

  • Abolished death penalty
  • Ban on juvenile death penalty
  • Ban on life-without-parole for minors
  • Major limits on life-without-parole sentences
  • Focus on rehabilitation over retribution in sentencing
  • Shorter average prison sentences
  • Lower incarceration rates
  • National standards for prison conditions
  • Ban or severe limits on private prisons
  • Limits on solitary confinement
  • Independent oversight of prisons
  • National police-oversight bodies
  • Mandatory national police standards
  • Standardized police training and certification
  • National reporting of police use-of-force incidents
  • Universal use of body cameras with clear rules
  • Decriminalization of many low-level offenses
  • National frameworks for restorative justice

Drugs & Public Health

  • National marijuana decriminalization or legalization
  • Nationwide harm-reduction strategies
  • Safe injection or supervised consumption sites
  • Needle-exchange programs
  • National opioid-response strategy
  • Emphasis on treatment over incarceration for drug use
  • Widespread availability of addiction treatment
  • Decriminalization of small-scale personal possession

Environment & Climate

  • National climate-change legislation
  • National greenhouse-gas reduction targets
  • National climate adaptation plans
  • Carbon taxes or emissions trading systems
  • Bans on many single-use plastics
  • Nationwide recycling infrastructure
  • National composting standards
  • Strict vehicle emissions standards
  • Strong fuel-efficiency standards
  • Coastal protection and managed retreat policies
  • Strict pesticide regulations
  • Strict water-quality protections
  • Strict air-quality standards
  • Strong wildlife and habitat protections
  • Strict logging and forestry regulations

Infrastructure & Transport

  • Nationwide high-speed rail networks
  • Nationalized or heavily regulated rail systems
  • Integrated national public-transport networks
  • Dense urban public-transit systems
  • Transit-oriented urban planning
  • Cycling infrastructure in cities
  • Pedestrian-first street design
  • Road-pricing or congestion-charging zones
  • Strict trucking regulations on hours and safety
  • Stricter driver-training and licensing
  • Periodic driver retesting for safety
  • National building and fire safety codes
  • Ban on for-profit fire departments
  • Modernized national power grids
  • Investment in resilient energy infrastructure

Digital Rights & Technology

  • National digital privacy laws
  • GDPR-style data-protection regimes
  • Limits on corporate data collection
  • Requirements for data minimization and purpose limitation
  • Right of access to personal data
  • Right to erasure of personal data
  • Restrictions on data transfer to third parties
  • Strict rules for government surveillance
  • Public or regulated digital identity systems
  • National electronic ID cards
  • Universal broadband access goals
  • Public investment in rural broadband
  • Net-neutrality protections
  • Right-to-repair regulations
  • Regulation of big-tech monopolies
  • Algorithmic transparency rules
  • Rules on automated decision-making affecting citizens

Finance, Consumer Protection & Housing

  • Caps on credit-card interest rates
  • Consumer-friendly overdraft rules
  • Regulation of bank fees
  • Strict payday-lending regulations
  • Limits on predatory lending
  • National medical-debt protections
  • Easier personal bankruptcy for individuals
  • Stronger tenant protections
  • Rent-control or rent-stabilization policies
  • National housing-affordability strategies
  • Robust public or social housing programs
  • Restrictions on speculative property ownership
  • Taxes or limits on empty investment properties
  • Inclusionary zoning requirements
  • Reforms reducing exclusive single-family zoning

Taxation & Economic Policy

  • Higher top marginal income tax rates
  • Significant wealth taxes
  • Robust inheritance taxes
  • Taxation of large estates
  • National value-added taxes to fund welfare states
  • Corporate tax enforcement with few loopholes
  • Capital-gains taxes closer to income-tax levels
  • Financial-transaction taxes
  • Green taxes on pollution and emissions
  • National industrial strategies
  • Public investment in strategic sectors
  • Strong antitrust enforcement
  • Limits on corporate concentration and monopolies

Immigration & Asylum

  • Long-standing national immigration frameworks
  • Clear legal pathways for skilled workers
  • Clear pathways for low-wage workers
  • Guest-worker programs with rights protections
  • National asylum systems with standardized procedures
  • Humane reception centers for asylum seekers
  • Integration and language-education programs
  • Pathways to permanent residency and citizenship
  • Voting rights for permanent residents in some elections
  • Family-reunification frameworks

Media, Information & Public Institutions

  • Strong public-service broadcasters
  • Stable, nonpartisan funding for public media
  • Media-ownership concentration limits
  • Political-advertising rules on TV and radio
  • Publicly funded fact-checking bodies
  • Independent national statistical agencies
  • High trust in national statistical data
  • Independent public health agencies
  • High trust in public-health institutions
  • Independent scientific advisory councils
  • Transparency laws for government records
  • National ombudsman institutions

Culture, Civic Life & Miscellaneous

  • Robust public funding for arts and culture
  • Public funding for museums and cultural institutions
  • Protected cultural-heritage policies
  • National civic-education curricula
  • Broad-based civic-literacy initiatives
  • Voting rights for ex-prisoners
  • In many cases, voting rights for prisoners
  • Protected time off for jury duty
  • Protected time off for civic duties
  • Stricter fireworks regulations
  • Strict national food-safety standards
  • Strict food-labeling requirements
  • Restrictions on certain food additives
  • Stricter animal-welfare standards
  • Restrictions on factory farming practices
  • National pet-ownership regulations in cities

This list is intentionally broad and simplified. Not every country does every item, and details vary, but most rich democracies have settled, stable policies in many of these areas, while the United States continues to debate, fragment, or resist them.
